### Conditions
  - __Acid burn__ (2 turns)
    - Deals 1-3 damage per turn.
  - __Bleeding__
  - __Burning__ (2 turns)
    - Deals 1-3 damage per turn.
    - Prevent using most abilities and secondaries. 
  - __Disoriented__ (2 Turns)
    - Apply stats penalty (-20 AIM/-6 Mobility).
    - Removes __Overwatch__ and __Suppression__.
    - Prevent using most abilities and secondaries. 
    - Breakes __Mind Control__ and kills __Psy-Zombie__ on psionics.
  - __Poisoned__
    - Apply stats penalty (-30 AIM/-6 Mobility).
    - Deals 1 damage per turn.

  - __Smoke__ (3 Turns)
    - +20 Defence for ALL units within cloud. (Even Sparks/MECs????)
